ABOUT US

     CapsiGO is a restaurant search and discovery portal along with social media and exclusive blogger interface.

     CapsiGO is an android based application that has a minimum requirement of Android 5.0(Lollipop). The main objective of the app is to find food nearby your location and also the application allows you to order food at your place. The application has got a social media interface where users are able to post photos and view photos from other users. The application provides an exclusive social media interface for the bloggers to post their reviews about the restaurants they visit.

     The main objective of this application is for the user to find good food in his nearby places and also he gets reviews about the place from BLOGS and is able to wisely choose his choices. The bloggers get prestige points according to the number of hits, number of burps(like) their blogs get.

     Aim of the system if to place orders at food outlets so that restaurants can deliver a better service to the user's location. The users are also satisfied as the food is delivered at their doorstep.
